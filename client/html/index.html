Fusion Palo Alto Teaching Assistant Video Demonstration

The video above displays a few of the capabilities of the teaching assistant integrated into Fusion Palo Alto. The teaching assistant's primary purpose is to assist with classroom related functions but the device also acts as a conduit between staff and students.
Interaction include, but are not limited to:

  • Greeting and learning about students in the classroom
  • Introducing students to each other using learned information (e.g. "Have you met [other student] before? They also like [learned detail]!")
  • Creating media from multiple students' input (i.e. poems, short stories, etc)
  • Interacting with other departments on the campus (e.g. playing student or teacher compositions from the music room)
This teaching assistant was built using the Amazon Alexa and interfaces with a NodeJS backend and an ElectronJS desktop application.
